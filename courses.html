Courses

Engineering

Engineering stands out as a top choice for international students seeking lucrative career paths. With its promising job market and competitive salaries, Engineering holds significant appeal, especially among Indian students.

Management

The management courses can be pursued at undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral levels, and may also be available as diplomas or certificates. Each course offers a unique set of skills and knowledge tailored to specific industries and career paths.

Information Technology

There is a diverse range of information technology (IT) courses offered by universities and colleges. These courses may vary in focus, specialization, and level of study (undergraduate, postgraduate, or doctoral).

Medicine

Medical courses are experiencing significant demand among international students due to their potential for establishing a strong foothold in the highly competitive field.

Accounting & Finance

Accounting and finance courses are highly sought-after disciplines among international students worldwide. These fields offer abundant job opportunities in various sectors of the market.

Hospitality & Tourism

Hospitality courses are offered by numerous universities and colleges, catering to individuals interested in pursuing careers in the hospitality industry.

Humanities & Social Science

The scope of Humanities and Social Science courses is broad and varied, offering students a rich academic experience and diverse career opportunities.

Fashion Designing

These courses cover various aspects of the fashion industry, including design, marketing, management, communication, textiles, retailing, styling, buying, illustration, journalism, and technology.
