About
Studycoach

Despite being a recent establishment, Studycoach is led by a founder and managing director who embarked on his own journey as an international student over 20 years ago. Drawing from his own personal experiences, we intimately understand the challenges faced by students navigating foreign educational systems. Leveraging this firsthand knowledge, we have since assisted numerous students in realizing their aspirations and pursuing their dreams.

Our Services

Personalized 1-on-1 guidance

Our dedicated team is readily available to provide premier career counselling services to students 24x7. Our team excels in helping students pinpoint courses and universities that align perfectly with their aptitude, interests, academic background, and financial resources.

Visa Application Assistance

Our team diligently scrutinizes every small detail, ensuring comprehensive attention to all aspects of the visa process. Additionally, our Managing Director, boasting 17 years of experience abroad, personally inspects each application to provide added assurance.

Educational Loan Assistance

Our team specializes in offering reliable advice and assistance in securing education loans for students planning to study abroad. We maintain close partnerships with banks that specialize in providing loans tailored to the needs of international students.

Airport Pick -up assistance

Our airport pick-up assistance is designed to provide a seamless and stress-free experience for those arriving in unfamiliar destinations. It offers peace of mind knowing that you will be met upon arrival and taken care of from the moment you land until you reach your destination.

Accommodation assistance

Our team can assist in finding the accommodation options across all budgets and preferences, including hotels, shared accommodation, and homestays. These accommodations are conveniently located near students' chosen institutions, ensuring easy access to basic provisions and essential infrastructure.

OET CBT OSCE Training for Nurses

The Occupational English Test (OET) is specifically designed for healthcare professionals, including nurses, who are looking to work or study in English-speaking environments. Our trainers are highly qualified and registered nurses of UK.
